Global Health Intelligence

Diabetes Mellitus

A chronic condition affecting how your body turns food into energy, characterized by high blood glucose levels.

Risk Factors Obesity, sedentary lifestyle, family history, and high blood pressure.
Common Symptoms Increased thirst, frequent urination, fatigue, and blurred vision.

Hypertension

Commonly known as high blood pressure, this condition increases the force of blood against your artery walls.

Risk Factors High salt intake, stress, smoking, and lack of physical activity.
Common Symptoms Often asymptomatic (The Silent Killer), but can cause headaches or chest pain.

Heart Disease

A range of conditions that affect your heart's ability to function correctly, including coronary artery disease.

Risk Factors High cholesterol, diabetes, smoking, and age.
Common Symptoms Shortness of breath, chest discomfort, and numbness in limbs.

Lung Cancer

A malignant tumor that starts in the lungs, often linked to long-term exposure to inhaled toxins.

Risk Factors Smoking (primary), secondhand smoke, and occupational exposure.
Common Symptoms Persistent cough, coughing blood, and unexplained weight loss.

Breast Cancer

Cancer that forms in the cells of the breasts, most common in women but can occur in men.

Risk Factors Genetic mutations (BRCA1/BRCA2), age, and dense breast tissue.
Common Symptoms Lump in the breast, skin changes (dimpling), or unusual discharge.
